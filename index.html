About Me
Hi, my name is Albert Linda. I am a Ph.D. student currently enrolled at IIT Kanpur, working under the supervision of Dr. Somnath Bhowmick. I work in computational material science, specifically on planer defects of metal and alloys. My work mainly consists of understanding the role of various internal and external parameters on them. For this, I use atomistic simulation techniques like density functional theory, molecular dynamics, and microstructural modeling technique like phase field.
